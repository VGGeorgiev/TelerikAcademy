2. set of communications protocols used
3. for the Internet and similar networks,
4. and generally the most popular
5. protocol stack for wide area networks. 
6. It is commonly known as TCP/IP,
7. because of its most important protocols:
8. Transmission Control Protocol 
9. (TCP) and Internet Protocol (IP), which 
10. were the first networking 
11. protocols defined in this standard. 
12. It is occasionally known as the DoD 
13. model due to the foundational 
14. influence of the ARPANET in the 1970s 
15. (operated by DARPA, an agency of the 
16. United States Department of Defense).
